# ResearchHub

> ResearchHub is an open science platform where researchers publish, peer review, fund, and discuss scientific research. Contributors earn ResearchCoin (RSC) for their work.

ResearchHub's mission is to accelerate the pace of scientific research. It operates as "a GitHub for science" — a collaborative platform for publishing preprints, conducting open peer review, funding research, and discussing findings in topic-specific communities called Hubs. The platform compensates peer reviewers ($150 per review), publishes content open access, and completes peer review in as few as 14 days. User-contributed content (excluding peer reviews) is licensed under CC BY 4.0. Maintained by the ResearchHub Foundation and ResearchHub, Inc.
